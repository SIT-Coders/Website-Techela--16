CodeRush

This one will test all your coding skills to the limit. Can you debug? Can you blind code? Can you CodeRush?

PuzzleMania

This event is designed to test your all round coding skills. Built over three rounds, this will stretch you to your limits.
-Allowed Languages: C, C++, Java
Round 1: Blind Code
In this round, participants will be provided with a problem to code. The constraint in this round is that the screen will be turned off.
Participants will be allowed to turn on the screen for only once.
Time Limit: 5 - 10 minutes
Round 2: Debugging
In this round, participants will be given 4 problems to debug in a stipulated time. The bugs may be logical, syntactical etc.
Round 3: Code Swap
In this round, if there are X participants, X/2 teams are made. Each team is provided with a set of 2 problems to solve (1 for each member).
After half time, the problems will be swapped between the two members of the same team and they solve the problem. Team members are not allowed to discuss among themselves in this round.
Time Limit: 20 - 30 minutes
-Automatic code checking software will be used. All results shall be considered final.
Entry Fee: Rs 150 for a team of 2, Rs 100 for an individual (pairs to be made on spot)
Prize: Rs 2000
